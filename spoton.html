SpotOn Output Classes
This system outputs only four classes: MEL, SCC, BCC, and OTHER. Educational examples do not change app output categories.
The ABCDE Rule
A
Asymmetry
One half doesn't match the other in shape or size
B
Border
Edges are ragged, notched, irregular, or blurred
C
Color
Multiple shades of brown, black, red, white, or blue
D
Diameter
Larger than 6 mm — about the size of a pencil eraser
E
Evolving
Any change in size, shape, color, or new symptom
UV Protection · Philippines
Use SPF 30+ Sunscreen Daily
The Philippines has a UV Index of 8–11 year-round. Reapply every 2 hours when outdoors.
Avoid Peak UV Hours
Stay in the shade between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM when radiation is strongest.
Wear Protective Clothing
Long sleeves, wide-brimmed salakot hats, and UV-blocking sunglasses are essential.
Monthly Self-Examination
Check all sun-exposed areas — face, neck, forearms, and upper back — every 30 days.
